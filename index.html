Privacy Policy for Vizio TV Remote App

Effective Date: July 24, 2025

1. Versions of the App

This app is available in two versions:

  • Paid Version (no ads)
  • Free Version (supported by ads)
This Privacy Policy applies to both versions, with key differences outlined below.

2. Data Collection

Paid Version:
This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No advertising or analytics services are used.

Free Version:
The free version uses Google AdMob to display ads.
AdMob may collect and use certain device information (such as device ID, IP address, and app usage data) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.
No personally identifiable information is collected by us directly.

3. Network Access

Both versions of the app access your local Wi-Fi network to discover and communicate with your Vizio Smart TV. This communication happens locally, and no data is sent to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Paid Version: No third-party services are used.

Free Version: Uses Google AdMob for advertisements. You can review AdMob’s privacy practices here.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page and reflected in the Google Play store listing.
